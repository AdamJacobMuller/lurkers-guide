Welcome to voice chat!

On this page you can chat live with other B5 fans about the latest episode, your thoughts about the show in general, or whatever else is on your mind -- no typing required. Just click the "Talk" button in the control panel on the right side of the page, grab your PC's microphone, and start speaking your mind.

Some tips and tricks:

  • The first time you visit this page, your browser will automatically download and install a small piece of software. In most cases it'll ask you if you want to allow the software to be installed -- you have to say yes or you won't be able to chat.
  • To change your name or use text chat, click on the "Click here for options" message near the bottom of the control panel and a menu will appear.
  • If you want to ignore a particular user, click on his or her name with your right mouse button and choose "Ignore."
  • You can hold down the "F9" key on your keyboard to talk if you're more partial to keyboards than mice.

For now the chat feature only works on Windows 95/98/NT under Netscape 4.0 or higher or Internet Explorer 4.0 or higher.

The chat software is VoicePresence from HearMe. You can add it to your Web pages too -- see HearMe's site for details.

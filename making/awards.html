Awards

See Also: [Emmy Award home page]
  • 1993 - Emmy award for Special Visual Effects for "The Gathering".
  • March, 1994 - Space Frontier Foundation award for "Best Vision of the Future."
  • 1994 - Emmy award for makeup for "The Parliament of Dreams".
  • July 1995 - Emmy nominations
    • Cinematography: "The Geometry of Shadows"
    • Makeup: "Acts of Sacrifice"
    • Hairstyling: "The Geometry of Shadows"
  • July 1996 - Emmy nomination for cinematography: "Comes the Inquisitor"
  • September 1996 - Hugo Award for Best Dramatic Presentation: "The Coming of Shadows"
  • October 1996 - Sci-Fi Universe awards for Best Series, Best Actor (Bruce Boxleitner,) Best Supporting Actor (Peter Jurasik,) and Best Supporting Actress (Mira Furlan.)
  • October 1996 - Space Frontier Foundation "Visions of the Future" award.
  • December 1996 - American Cinema Foundations's 1997 E Pluribus Unum Award for "Passing Through Gethsemane."
  • July 1997 - Emmy nomination for best makeup: "The Summoning"
  • September 1997 - Hugo award for Best Dramatic Presentation: "Severed Dreams"

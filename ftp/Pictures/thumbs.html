Babylon 5 Pictures: Thumbnail Images

This area contains various images from and about Babylon 5. The images are divided into four categories:

Characters
Pictures of the people and aliens of Babylon 5.
Effects
Outer-space scenes and other effects shots.
Misc
Miscellaneous pictures (e.g. advertisements).
Scenes
Scenes from specific episodes.

You should strongly consider turning on delayed image loading, if your browser supports such a feature, since each of these pages contains lots of miniature pictures and loading them all at once usually takes a while.
